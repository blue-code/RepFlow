RepFlow is laser-focused on two exercises: push-ups and pull-ups. Auto-count via Apple Watch motion sensors, and grow fast with the proven GTG (Grease the Groove) method.

▷ GTG MODE ⭐ The differentiator no other app delivers right
Popularized by Pavel Tsatsouline, GTG is the proven method for progressing on simple bodyweight exercises. Spread light reps (RPE 5) throughout the day; your nervous system adapts and your max reps grow rapidly. RepFlow automates this via watch notifications.

▷ Watch Motion Auto-Count
Real-time push-up/pull-up cycle detection from the watch's accelerometer + gyroscope. Runs in the background even when the screen is off, with haptic feedback every rep.

▷ Intelligent Interval Trainer
EMOM (every minute), Tabata (20s/10s × 8), and AMRAP (max reps in time). Watch haptics signal round transitions.

▷ Local-First
All workout data lives on your device. No external servers. No ads. No analytics SDKs.

▷ HealthKit Integration
Completed workouts auto-save to the Health app. Live heart rate on the watch during workouts.

- Heart rate + active calories
